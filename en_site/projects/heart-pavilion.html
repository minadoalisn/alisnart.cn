Project: Twilight Heart-Lit Pavilion

Cultural Tourism

Landscape Lighting

Night Economy

A romantic, high-end Cultural Tourism installation designed to enhance the night-time economy. This structure combines natural elements (thatched roof) with advanced LED lighting to create a stunning visual focal point.

1. Design & Lighting Specs

StructureTropical Thatched Roof + Reinforced Frame
LightingCustom Heart-Shaped Crystal Mesh + LED Strip Outlining
AmbienceWarm Golden Tone (3000K) + Floor Uplighting
ApplicationResort Lounges, Scenic Spots, Night Markets

2. Key Features

Custom Heart Motifs: Three large, glowing heart shapes suspended from the center beam, crafted from wireframe mesh filled with thousands of fairy lights.

Integrated Outline Lighting: Hidden LED strips trace the eaves and pillars, creating a "lantern" effect that casts a warm glow on the surrounding patio.

Organic Leaf Lamps: Custom leaf-shaped floor lamps at the base add a touch of nature and organic design.

3. Impact

This project demonstrates our ability to create immersive, romantic environments. Perfect for driving Night Economy by turning simple seating areas into viral social media spots.
