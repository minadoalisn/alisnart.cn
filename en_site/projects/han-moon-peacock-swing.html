Project: Han Moon Peacock Swing

Cultural Tourism Lighting

Interactive Installation

Night Tourism Photo Spot

Han-Style Design

A 3.5m×2.8m giant tilted rectangular frame with integrated swing seating, decorated with traditional Han-style cloud patterns, golden filigree ornaments, a peacock tail feather sculpture extending 1.8m along the right side, and mountain-shaped fiberglass base formations. LED strips outline the frame contour, warm uplighting illuminates the seating area, 8-12 floating LED orbs create a dreamlike atmosphere, and an ultrasonic fog system generates immersive mist. Designed for night tourism scenic spots, ancient towns, and cultural tourism commercial streets. Covers 6m×4m footprint with 2-3 days on-site installation.

1. Design Concept

The centerpiece is a 3.5m tall × 2.8m wide tilted rectangular frame in dark blue finish. The top-left corner features traditional Chinese cloud motifs with golden filigree flower ornaments and a warm-glow paper lantern (40cm diameter, 2700K). The top-right transitions into a sweeping peacock tail feather extending 1.8m downward. The base consists of glossy blue mountain-shaped formations in fiberglass, evoking traditional Chinese shan shui landscape aesthetics. A white swing seat with metal chains sits inside the frame, inviting visitors to sit and take photos.

2. Lighting System

LED strips (6000K white) outline the entire frame contour. Warm uplighting from the floor illuminates the seating area. 8-12 floating LED orbs (3000K warm white, 10-15cm diameter) create a firefly-like effect inside the frame. The traditional lantern houses a warm 2700K light source. All lighting is controlled via smart controller with adjustable brightness and animation modes.

Materials & Craftsmanship

Carbon steel frame with welded joints and deep-blue baked paint finish. Decorative elements (clouds, peacock tail, mountains) hand-sculpted in fiberglass (FRP) with hand-painted color detailing. All components factory pre-fabricated for quick on-site assembly.

Application Scenarios

Scenic area entrances, ancient town bridges/plazas, cultural tourism commercial streets, theme park interactive zones, shopping mall atriums/outdoor plazas, park lakesides, festival lighting events. Requires 220V/3kW power connection and hardened ground surface.

Technical Specifications

  • Frame dimensions: 3.5m (H) × 2.8m (W) × 0.3m (D)
  • Footprint: 6m × 4m (including mountain base formations)
  • Frame material: Carbon steel welded construction, deep-blue baked paint, wind resistance level 10
  • Decorative elements: Hand-sculpted fiberglass (FRP), UV-resistant color finish ≥5 years
  • Lighting: LED strips (6000K) + uplighting + 8-12 floating orbs + lantern (2700K)
  • Protection rating: IP65 outdoor waterproof, dual waterproof electrical protection
  • Fog system: Ultrasonic atomization module, 3-5m coverage diameter
  • Power requirement: 220V / 3kW, single power outlet
  • Installation: Factory pre-fabrication 10 days + on-site assembly 2-3 days
  • Operating temperature: -15°C to 45°C

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this type of interactive lighting installation cost?

A 3.5m frame with swing, LED lighting system, and fiberglass sculpture costs approximately $3,000-10,000 USD depending on configuration, sculpture complexity, and lighting control features.

What is the waterproof rating?

Outdoor version uses IP65 protection rating, suitable for heavy rain. Electrical systems use waterproof junction boxes and sealant. Fiberglass sculptures are UV-resistant and fade-proof.

How long does installation take?

Can the installation be customized with different themes?

Yes. We offer Han-style, fantasy, ocean, starry sky, and other themed customization. The frame structure is standardized while decorative elements and lighting programming are interchangeable.

How does the artificial fog system work?

The fog system uses ultrasonic atomization modules, each covering a 3-5m diameter. Built-in water tank runs 4-6 hours continuously with timer control. Integrated with the lighting system.
