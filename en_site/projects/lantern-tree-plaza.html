Project: 华灯树广场

Commercial Street Theme Lighting Display

A large tree-form lighting installation with LED-wrapped trunk and branches, traditional-style lanterns and fiber optic orbs suspended in the canopy. Ideal for commercial streets, festival displays, and urban plaza lighting.

1. Design Concept

Tree-form steel structure with bark-textured finish. 3.5m canopy diameter with 20-40 lanterns and LED orbs of varying sizes. Circular bench at the base for visitors to rest and take photos. Combines traditional lantern aesthetics with modern lighting control.

2. Lighting System

LED strips wrap the trunk and branches (warm white 2700K). Canopy features lanterns (warm yellow 2200K) + fiber optic orbs (RGB programmable). Supports timer, brightness adjustment, and dynamic mode switching.

Materials & Craftsmanship

Q235 carbon steel welded frame with anti-rust primer and bark-textured topcoat. Lanterns use PC rain covers + LED modules, weather-resistant >=5 years. Circular bench in treated wood + stainless steel frame.

Application Scenarios

Ideal for commercial pedestrian streets, urban plazas, festival displays, ancient town main streets, theme parks, and mall outdoor spaces.

Technical Specifications

  • Footprint: 5m x 5m (including bench area)
  • Height: 4.5m (including canopy)
  • Canopy diameter: 3.5m
  • Frame: Q235 carbon steel, bark-textured baked paint
  • Lanterns: 20-40 pieces (various sizes, PC rain covers)
  • Lighting: LED strips (warm white 2700K) + lanterns (2200K) + fiber orbs (RGB)
  • Protection: IP65 outdoor waterproof
  • Seating: Circular treated wood bench, stainless steel frame
  • Power: 220V / 3kW
  • Installation: Factory 8 days + on-site 1-2 days
