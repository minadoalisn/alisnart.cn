Project: Dragon Cloud Light Sculpture

Cultural Tourism Lighting

Interactive Installation

Landmark Sculpture

This project showcases our capability in Large public art fabrication. The "Dragon Cloud" is a 20-meter high interactive light sculpture designed for a major cultural tourism park in China.

1. Project Background

The client required a landmark sculpture that would serve as the centerpiece of their night-time economy zone. The sculpture needed to be weather-resistant, visually striking from a distance, and interactive for visitors.

2. Specifications & Data

Dimensions Height: 20m | Span: 15m | Weight: 12 Tons
Material 304 Stainless Steel + UV-Resistant PC Cover
Lighting System 50,000 RGB LED pixels | DMX512 Control | IP65 Waterproof
Structure Internal Steel Skeleton (Q345B) | Wind Load Resistance: Level 12

3. Fabrication Process

Step 1: 3D Modeling & Simulation. We used Rhino and Grasshopper to model the complex curve and simulate wind loads.

Step 2: Internal Framework. CNC cutting and welding of the main steel skeleton. Anti-corrosion treatment applied.

Step 3: Surface Cladding. Precision cutting and welding of stainless steel panels. Polishing to a mirror finish.

Step 4: Lighting Integration. LED modules installed inside the hollow structure. Programming of light shows.

4. Installation & Delivery

On-site Assembly: The sculpture was pre-assembled in our factory, then disassembled into 8 modules for shipping.
Overseas Shipping: We provide flat-rack container shipping for oversized items.
Installation: Our engineering team flew to the site to supervise the assembly and commissioning (7 days).

5. Conclusion

The Dragon Cloud sculpture has become a viral spot for tourists, increasing night-time foot traffic by 40%. This project proves our ability as a Custom stainless steel sculpture factory to deliver complex, high-quality projects on time.
