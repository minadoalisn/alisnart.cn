Chinoiserie Geometric Light Gate

48|

Interactive Immersive Light Art Installation

49| 50|
51|

The Chinoiserie Geometric Light Gate is an innovative light installation that integrates traditional Chinese culture, modern technology, and interactive experience. It features a 4.5-meter-high Q345 steel structure, complemented by laser-engraved acrylic light guide plates and custom RGB full-color LED strips. Equipped with infrared sensing and sound control systems, it dynamically responds to pedestrian flow or sound changes. The installation covers an area of 9m×6m, boasts IP67 protection, and has a weather resistance of 10 years. It is suitable for various scenarios such as urban squares, scenic entrances, and commercial streets, offering visitors a unique immersive interactive experience.

Design Concept

57|

Fusing traditional Chinese geometric patterns with modern minimalist design, it creates a profound visual space through multi-layered structures and transparent materials. Light and shadow flow and transform between geometric lines, symbolizing the continuous vitality and innovative development of traditional culture. The installation boasts a grand appearance and exquisite details, embodying both artistic aesthetics and technological sophistication.

Interactive Experience

61|

Equipped with a highly sensitive infrared sensor array, it accurately captures pedestrian trajectories, achieving a dynamic "light follows movement" effect. Concurrently, the integrated sound control module allows the light and shadow to rhythmically pulsate with ambient sounds (such as music or applause), creating an immersive auditory and visual feast. Multiple interactive modes can be programmed and switched to provide customized experiences for different scenarios.

Structure & Materials

68|

The main body is precisely welded from high-strength Q345 steel and treated with a three-layer fluorocarbon coating to effectively resist erosion from extreme weather. The light-emitting area uses 10mm thick high-transparency PMMA acrylic light guide plates, laser-engraved to form a uniform light-guiding dot matrix, ensuring soft and layered lighting effects. All connectors are made of 304 stainless steel, guaranteeing long-term stability and safety for outdoor use.

Lighting & Control

72|

The core light source consists of custom high-density RGB full-color LED strips, featuring 256 grayscale levels and 16 million color adjustment capabilities. An Art-Net distributed control system is adopted to achieve independent programming and precise control of each LED bead. It has over 30 preset light and shadow animation effects, supporting remote debugging and timed on/off, meeting diverse scene requirements.
