Project: Biomorphic LED Canopy

Commercial Plaza Lighting

LED Art Installation

Biomorphic Design

Smart Lighting

1. Design Concept

The canopy features a wavy, undulating roof that curves upward at the edges, supported by three flared pillars. The overall form is organic and flowing, resembling a stylized metallic tree. The base uses a perforated honeycomb lattice that glows with soft white light, creating a floating visual effect.

2. Lighting & Programming

Dual-color dynamic lighting in neon green and magenta. Patterns include V-shaped radiating lines and nested diamond geometric motifs. The center features a small green cross inside a diamond. LED strips are embedded in the canopy bottom for an immersive experience.

3. Application Scenarios

Ideal for urban commercial plazas, pedestrian streets, outdoor atriums of shopping malls, public art spaces, and cultural tourism scenic spots. Can serve as an independent landmark or a series installation.
