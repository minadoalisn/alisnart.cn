Project: 天环广场

Giant Mountain Ring Lighting Art Landmark

A 10-15m giant polished stainless steel ring standing in an alpine meadow, its mirror surface reflecting the sky and surrounding mountains. Inner ring features carved cloud and wave relief patterns. At night, LED strips outline the ring's contour, creating a breathtaking fusion of nature and light art. Ideal for scenic mountain areas, national parks, and resort destinations.

1. Design Concept

The centerpiece is a massive torus (ring) 10-15m in diameter, crafted from mirror-polished stainless steel. The outer surface is highly reflective, capturing the sky, clouds, and mountain landscape. The inner rim features CNC-carved relief patterns (spiral clouds/wave motifs), with select areas electroplated in gold/bronze contrasting with the silver base. The ring rests on a circular paved platform with a stone pathway leading to its center. By day, a mirror art sculpture; by night, an illuminated landmark.

2. Lighting System

LED strips (RGBW full-color) embedded along the ring base and inner rim, programmable via DMX512 protocol. Supports gradient, breathing, chasing, and other dynamic modes. Auxiliary ground uplights enhance the ring's 3D form. IP65 rated for outdoor weather resistance.

Materials & Craftsmanship

304/316L mirror-polished stainless steel, 8-12mm wall thickness, with internal carbon steel skeleton. Relief patterns CNC-carved, hand-polished, and electroplated (silver/gold/bronze options). Foundation: reinforced concrete + stone paving. Wind resistance level 12, seismic resistance level 8.

Application Scenarios

Ideal for scenic mountain areas, national parks, resort destination plazas, and large outdoor landmarks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this giant ring installation cost?

Depending on size and craftsmanship, a 10m diameter standard version costs approximately $700,000-2,000,000 RMB. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Will the mirror stainless steel rust outdoors?

304/316L food-grade mirror stainless steel with 8-12mm wall thickness and electroplated corrosion treatment provides ≥20 years outdoor weather resistance without rust. 316L recommended for coastal environments.

What is the wind and seismic resistance?

Internal carbon steel skeleton + reinforced concrete foundation, wind resistance level 12, seismic level 8. Ring is anchored to the platform with foundation bolts.
