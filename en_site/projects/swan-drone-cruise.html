Autonomous Swan Drone Show

Intelligent Water Surface Light Art Installation

Alisnart Optoelectronics proudly introduces the autonomous swan drone series for water surface light shows, seamlessly blending traditional artistic aesthetics with cutting-edge unmanned vessel technology. Each cruising swan integrates a high-precision GPS positioning module and an intelligent obstacle avoidance system, enabling precise navigation and complex formation performances. The hull is crafted from advanced corrosion-resistant composite materials and equipped with RGBW full-color LED lighting modules. A central control system ensures synchronized light and music interactions. Each swan drone measures 3.5 meters in length, boasts IP68 waterproof rating, and has an endurance of 6-8 hours, delivering unprecedented visual impact and interactive experiences for urban parks, tourist attractions, and large-scale celebratory events.

Intelligent Navigation System

Features an integrated high-precision RTK-GPS positioning system, achieving centimeter-level accuracy (±0.5 meters). Equipped with a multi-sensor fusion intelligent obstacle avoidance module (ultrasonic + visual) that detects water surface obstacles in real-time, ensuring safe navigation. Supports custom route planning, fixed-point hovering, circling targets, and various other cruising modes. Can be launched and task-switched with a single click via a remote control platform (PC/APP).

Multi-Unit Formation & Light Show

Alisnart's independently developed cluster control algorithm supports coordinated formation performances with over 100 drones. The lighting system utilizes DMX512/Art-Net protocols, with thousands of built-in RGBW full-color lighting animation effects that can be precisely synchronized with music rhythms, presenting dynamic light and shadow patterns such as waves, graphics, and text. Each LED bead has a brightness of 200 lumens, ensuring smooth color transitions and strong visual impact.

Hull Design & Materials

The hull measures 3.5m in length, 1.8m in width, and 1.5m in height (including the swan shape). It is integrally molded from high-strength ASA composite material, offering excellent weather resistance, UV resistance, and corrosion resistance, making it suitable for both freshwater and saltwater environments. The modular design facilitates easy transportation, installation, and maintenance. The hull bottom is equipped with efficient ducted thrusters, with cruising noise below 40 decibels, minimizing environmental impact on aquatic ecosystems.

Energy & Safety Assurance

A built-in high-performance lithium battery pack (24V/100Ah) provides 6-8 hours of extended endurance. Supports automatic recharging via shore-based or underwater charging stations. Equipped with multiple safety protection mechanisms: low battery automatic return, safe shutdown on communication interruption, remote emergency stop, anti-rollover design, and IP68 highest-level waterproofing. All electrical interfaces use professional waterproof connectors.
