Project: Waterborne Drone Swan Cruise

Smart Light Art

Waterborne Drones

Night Tourism Tech

Eco-Friendly Performance

Redefining night tourism with a fusion of **aerial drone technology** and **aquatic art installations**. The Waterborne Drone Swan is an intelligent, autonomous light sculpture that performs choreographed shows on water surfaces.

1. Technical Specifications

Design Concept Realistic Swan Geometry with Dynamic Feather Animation
Propulsion System Silent Waterborne Drone Technology (Electric/Hybrid)
Lighting Effects High-Brightness LED Mapping + Projection Capability
Control System Centralized Fleet Control / App Programming / Automated Paths
Waterproof Rating IP68 (Marine Grade Materials)
Battery Life Up to 4-6 Hours Continuous Cruise / 2 Hours Performance Mode

2. Performance Capabilities

Choreographed Shows: Multiple swans can perform synchronized formations, creating stunning visual patterns on lakes, rivers, or resort pools.

Interactive Interaction: Optional voice-activated modes or sensor-based interactions where swans "follow" visitors along the water edge.

Eco-Friendly: Zero emissions, silent operation—perfect for nature reserves and high-end eco-tourism resorts.

3. Why It's a Night Tourism Game-Changer

Traditional boat shows are expensive to operate. The **Waterborne Drone Swan** offers:

  • ✅ Lower Operational Cost: Fully automated, minimal staff needed.
  • ✅ Visual Novelty: Combines the romance of swans with the "wow" factor of drones.
  • ✅ Scalability: Can be deployed as a single centerpiece or a fleet of 10+ units.

4. Application Scenarios

Ideal for Lake Scenic Spots, Resort Water Features, Theme Park Parades, and City Light Festivals.
