Project: Holographic Low-Poly Whale

Public Art

Interactive Installations

Landmark Sculpture

A striking example of modern Interactive Installations. This massive, geometric whale sculpture features a low-poly aesthetic combined with advanced holographic materials that shift colors from deep purple to silver depending on the light angle.

1. Design & Fabrication Specs

StyleLow-Poly Geometric (Triangular Facets)
DimensionsLarge-Scale (Estimated ~8m Length)
Frame MaterialBlack Coated Steel / Galvanized Internal Structure
Surface MaterialHolographic Iridescent PC Panels (UV Resistant)

2. Manufacturing Process

Structural Integrity: The sculpture is built on a heavy-duty internal steel skeleton, ensuring stability for outdoor public art installation.

Precision Facets: Each triangular panel is CNC-cut and manually assembled to create the seamless "origami" look.

Color-Shifting Effect: We utilize high-grade iridescent films laminated onto durable polycarbonate sheets, ensuring the vibrant colors remain vivid for years outdoors.

3. Application

Ideal for Urban Park Art, shopping mall atriums, and cultural tourism landmarks. This sculpture serves as an instant photo-op and interactive attraction.
