Project: Tropical Resort Lighting Archway

Resort Entrance Lighting

Landscape Light Installation

Tropical Style

Hotel Lighting

1. Archway Structure

A massive, rounded archway with a deep emerald green outer frame. The upper spandrels feature an intricate dark reddish-brown lattice resembling radiating palm fronds. The inner tunnel is lined with warm, honey-colored vertical wood slats that glow with amber light.

2. Cylindrical Tower

To the right stands a tall cylindrical structure clad in vertical dark green fins. It is perforated with large, irregular leaf-shaped cutouts. Bright warm yellow light shines from within through these openings, creating a "breathing lantern" effect. The top edge is jagged, mimicking a forest canopy.

3. Application Scenarios

Perfect for high-end resort entrances, hotel landscape gates, SPA resort pathways, tropical theme parks, and cultural tourism scenic area entrances.
