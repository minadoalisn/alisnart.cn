Project: 月下仙境

Immersive Night Tourism Lighting Installation

A dreamlike immersive lighting installation inspired by traditional Chinese garden aesthetics. Features multi-layer fiber optic starlight array, ultrasonic fog system, and interactive floor projection. Designed for scenic night tourism, ancient towns, and cultural tourism plazas.

1. Design Concept

The 'Wonderland' theme features a multi-layer fiber optic array simulating a starry sky effect, with fog modules at the base creating layered cloud visuals. Interactive floor projection creates light ripples when visitors walk through. Modular prefabricated design for quick on-site assembly.

2. Lighting System

Fiber optic array (cool white + warm white gradient) + interactive floor projection (RGB full-color) + ultrasonic fog module (5-8m coverage) + suspended LED orbs (12-20 pieces, 10-20cm diameter). DMX512 protocol compatible.

Materials & Craftsmanship

Aluminum alloy frame + carbon steel connectors with matte black baked paint finish. PMMA high-transmittance fiber optics, UV-resistant. Floor projection area covered with anti-slip tempered glass, LED modules IP67 waterproof.

Application Scenarios

Ideal for commercial pedestrian streets, urban plazas, festival displays, ancient town main streets, theme parks, and mall outdoor spaces.

Technical Specifications

  • Footprint: 8m x 6m
  • Height: 3.0m (including suspended structure)
  • Frame: Aluminum alloy + carbon steel, wind resistance level 10
  • Fiber optics: PMMA high-transmittance, cool white + warm white gradient
  • Lighting control: DMX512 protocol, programming/timer/sensor modes
  • Floor projection: RGB full-color, anti-slip tempered glass cover
  • Fog system: Ultrasonic atomization, 5-8m coverage diameter
  • Protection: IP65 outdoor waterproof, dual waterproof electrical protection
  • Power: 220V / 5kW, 1-2 power outlets required
  • Installation: Factory 12 days + on-site 2-3 days

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this installation cost?

Standard version (8m x 6m) costs approximately $7,000-15,000 USD depending on fiber optic count, projection area, and fog system configuration.

What is the waterproof rating?

IP65 outdoor waterproof rating. Floor projection uses IP67 modules with tempered glass. Electrical systems have dual waterproof protection.

How long does installation take?

Factory pre-fabrication takes ~12 days. On-site assembly, wiring, and testing takes 2-3 days.

Can it be customized with different themes?

Yes. We offer Wonderland, starry sky, ocean, forest themes. Frame is standardized while lighting and decorative elements are interchangeable.
