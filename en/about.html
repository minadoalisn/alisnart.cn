About Alisn Art

We Know Traffic, We Know Engineering

We believe cultural tourism lighting is not just about beauty — it's about giving spaces new reasons for visitors to stay, share, and come back.

Company Profile

Guangdong Alisn Photoelectric Technology Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive technology service provider specializing in cultural tourism scene innovation and light art empowerment. We offer one-stop services from concept planning, visual design, installation fabrication, control systems to on-site commissioning.

Our services cover scenic areas, commercial spaces, urban public spaces, light festivals and large-scale stage events across China, helping projects transform nighttime traffic into memorable experiences.

Core Philosophy

We don't sell equipment — we deliver visitors' dwell time.

From "nobody cares" to "viral hotspot", the key is not filling the space with lights, but making light and shadow a tool for spatial narrative and operational growth.

Our Capabilities

A light project team must understand aesthetics, communication, construction and maintenance simultaneously.

Communication Expert

Every core installation is validated for photo angles, interactive participation and sharing paths.

Culture Expert

Extract visual motifs from local stories, reducing homogenized lighting products.

Engineering Expert

Focus on structural safety, waterproofing, lightning protection, control systems, power supply and maintenance.
