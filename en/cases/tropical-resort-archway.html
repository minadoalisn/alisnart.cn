Resort Entrance Lighting

Tropical Resort Archway

Palm leaf lattice with warm wooden tunnel — a signature entrance for high-end hotels and SPAs.

Project Highlights

  • Archway Structure: Deep emerald green outer frame with intricate palm leaf lattice.
  • Lighting Effects: Warm white (2700K) ambient glow, "breathing lantern" effect via leaf cutouts.
  • Application: High-end resort entrances, hotel landscape gates, SPA pathways.
  • Specs: IP65 Waterproof, UV & Moisture Resistant.
