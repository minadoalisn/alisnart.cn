Commercial Plaza Lighting

Biomorphic LED Canopy

Futuristic wavy roof with neon green and magenta dual-color dynamic lighting — a viral landmark for commercial plazas.

Project Highlights

  • Design Concept: Organic, flowing form resembling a stylized metallic tree with a perforated honeycomb base.
  • Lighting Effects: Neon green & magenta gradient, V-shaped radiating lines, and diamond motifs.
  • Application: Urban commercial plazas, pedestrian streets, outdoor atriums.
  • Specs: IP65 Waterproof, DMX512 Control, Wind Load Level 8.
